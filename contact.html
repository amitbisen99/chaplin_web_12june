Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Hindu chaplain?

A Hindu chaplain is a trained spiritual care provider who offers culturally and spiritually sensitive support rooted in Hindu traditions. Unlike temple priests who perform rituals, chaplains are trained to counsel individuals through personal crises, grief, and life transitions.

Do I need to be Hindu to receive services?

While our services are rooted in Hindu spiritual traditions, we welcome anyone seeking compassionate support. Interfaith understanding and respect for all spiritual paths are central to our approach.

How can I schedule a consultation?

You can reach us by phone at +1 (201) 757-2278, email at info@thehinduchaplain.com, or by filling out the contact form above. We offer both in-person and virtual consultations.

Is there a fee for chaplaincy services?

Many of our services are offered on a donation basis or are covered through institutional partnerships with hospitals and universities. Contact us to discuss your specific situation and needs.
