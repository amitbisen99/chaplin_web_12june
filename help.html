Our Commitment to You

Hindu Chaplaincy is dedicated to providing compassionate spiritual care and support. By submitting this request, you acknowledge the following:

1. Confidentiality: All information shared will be kept confidential and used solely for the purpose of providing spiritual care and support.

2. Nature of Support: Our services focus on spiritual guidance, emotional support, and connection to Hindu traditions. We are not a substitute for professional medical, legal, or mental health services.

3. Response Time: While we strive to respond to all requests within 24 hours, response times may vary based on the nature and urgency of requests.

4. Referrals: When appropriate, we may suggest additional resources or professionals who can assist with your needs.

We will respond to your request within 24 hours.
